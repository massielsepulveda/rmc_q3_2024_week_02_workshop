Summary of NCD Burden Findings

3 chosen from the Latin America and Caribean Region were chosen: Dominican Republic, Mexico and Peru.

The trend of NCD burden in Peru seems to be declining, while the one for Dominican Republic increases over time.

In the case of Mexico, there has been a slow decline that has become slower in recent years.
